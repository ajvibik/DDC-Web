Computing & Storage Solutions



Storage consumes significant part of all current IT spending and Tradewell has significant experience and best practice knowledge to work with you in creating cost-effective solutions that address your specific data storage and management challenges. We have a network of skilled professionals and intellectual capital that can provide you with quality services as per your business requirements. We offer a comprehensive portfolio of storage management services like advising on your options; to designing and deploying scalable, reliable, and efficient multi-vendor data storage infrastructures. We provide solutions that address current and future infrastructure needs by leveraging your existing storage assets while helping you deploy new networking storage technologies like Network Attached Storage which provide shared data to clients and servers on a LAN and reduce further management costs.

Database Management Services:

1. Proactive Monitoring – 24*7 monitoring, optional database clustering services, monitor availability and response time statistics.
2. Performance Management – ITIL based change and incident management, database installation and configuration & backup and disaster recovery planning.
3. Database Security Management
4. Fault Management – Real time diagnosis and database restoration
5. Back – up and Recovery – data backup, restore and deleted item retention, flexible storage options and control for the database.
